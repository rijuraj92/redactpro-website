FAQ
Common Questions
Is my data safe?
Yes. RedactPro runs 100% offline on your local machine. No documents are uploaded to any cloud service. Your files never leave your network.
Are the redactions permanent?
Yes. Redacted text is permanently removed from the exported PDFs using black-box overlays. The original text cannot be recovered from the exported files.
Can I use it for DSAR requests?
Absolutely. RedactPro was built specifically for DSAR compliance, litigation discovery, and FOI responses. The audit log provides defensible documentation of the redaction process.
Does it work with Purview exports?
Yes. Export your search results from Microsoft Purview eDiscovery, then import the entire export folder into RedactPro. It handles MSG files, EML files, and all common document formats.
What about scanned PDFs?
RedactPro includes built-in OCR powered by Tesseract. Scanned pages are automatically detected and processed. Text extraction accuracy depends on scan quality.
Can I try before buying?
Yes. The free trial gives you full access to all features for 14 days. No credit card required. No limitations on the number of files or exports during the trial.
